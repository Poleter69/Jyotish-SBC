Vedha Types Explained

Across vedha — horizontal or vertical line from transiting planet. Most effective when planet is stationary or at normal speed.


Fore vedha — clockwise diagonal. Effective when planet moves fast. Sun and Moon always cast fore vedha.


Hind vedha — anti-clockwise diagonal. Effective during retrograde. Rahu and Ketu always cast hind vedha.
